Terms of Service

The Airports Council International (ACI) COVID-19 Protocol Portal was developed by ACI as a service to the industry and the traveling public in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The portal allows airports to self-report airport measures, airport services, and passenger requirements. Airport participation is entirely voluntary.

ACI has developed and controls the Airport Programming Interface Portal ("the ACI COVID-19 Protocol") that enables airports to share their information using this online portal for third-party use. The ACI COVID-19 Protocol Portal is a data aggregator that enables third parties (such as airlines, travel apps, etc.) to access data for airport information in a standardized format using a single online portal.
