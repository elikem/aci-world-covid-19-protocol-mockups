Airport Health Measures

My Health Measure Portal

The Airport Council International (ACI) COVID-19 Protocol Portal was developed by ACI as service to the industry and the traveling public in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The portal allows airports to self-report airport measure, airport services, and passenger requirements. Following submission, airport responses will be reviewed and published to ACI's website upon approval. Airport participation is entirely voluntary.

The registering party should have the authority to submit information on behalf of the Airport.
